About Us

The Rain Flower brand fire extinguisher has been imported and distributed to Myanmar since 2001. In 2001, the showroom of fire extinguishers was successfully established in Yangon downtown area with delivery, installation and explanation the knowledges of fire safety to the public. And the other provinces were sold through the highway gates.

In 2003, the branch Showroom of Rain Flower Fire Extinguisher was opened in Mandalay. And in 2008, the branch Showroom was also opened in the country’s city, Naypyidaw. In 2013, another branch Showroom of Yangon was opened on the Pyay Road.

Beginning in 2016, the Rain Flower has improved the quality and upgraded the guarantee years into five years. In 2022, with a better quality of stainless steel Fire Extinguishers were imported and distributed.

Our Vision
